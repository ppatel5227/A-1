Yogi Adityanath

Biography

Yogi Adityanath, born on June 5, 1972, is an Indian politician and a monk who is serving as the 22nd and current Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh. He has been in office since March 19, 2017. Yogi Adityanath is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the founder of the Hindu Yuva Vahini, a youth organization. Before becoming Chief Minister, he was the Member of Parliament from the Gorakhpur constituency, serving five consecutive terms since 1998.

Achievements

Yogi Adityanath has been pivotal in numerous initiatives and reforms, including:

  • Implementation of the anti-conversion law
  • Development of infrastructure in Uttar Pradesh
  • Launching various welfare schemes for women and children
  • Improvement of law and order in the state
  • Promotion of religious tourism in Uttar Pradesh
